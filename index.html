    CRITERIA

    Industry Relevance & Innovative Business Models:

    We prioritise companies in the HealthTech, FinTech, and EntTech sectors, particularly those disrupting traditional paradigms through innovative business models and cutting-edge technologies. We remain open to transformative technologies that intersect with our core focus areas.

    Emerging Markets & Social Impact:

    We target businesses in emerging markets, seeing them as unique opportunities to unlock growth and innovation. Beyond financial returns, our investments aim for scalable solutions that contribute to societal wel-being and local economies.

    OUR ORIGIN

    Skalaban Capital was born from a shared dream, with Alex Skalaban and a team of savvy professionals at its core. Together, they realised that to truly grow, they needed more than big ideas — they needed a solid structure. Alex, with his track record in tech, joined the expertise within the group to bring our vision to life.

    We created Skalaban Capital as a hub where a dedicated management team takes care of business, allowing Alex to focus on the bigger picture.

    Here stands Skalaban Capital, an investment group launched with a mature perspective and rooted in a rich and extensive history, dedicated to discovering and nurturing the future leaders in Finance, Wellbeing, and Entertainment.
